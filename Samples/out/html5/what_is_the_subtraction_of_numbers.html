What is Subtraction?

To explain what it is that you are doing when you use the JPS subtraction feature.

To successfully perform the task of subtracting numbers, you should know what subtraction is. "Subtraction" is a mathematical concept in which you find the difference between two numbers.

So, the purpose of subtraction is to reduce a number into it's smaller form. We take one number away from the other.

The minus (-) symbol represents subtraction of numbers.

This same symbol is used in the app to represent subtraction; therefore, it will complete the task of subtracting numbers.
